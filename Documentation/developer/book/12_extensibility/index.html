Plug-in Renderers

Required Geometries

Plug-in renderers are required to support the following geometry types:

  • Triangle
  • Line
  • Point

Under QD3D, plug-in renderers were also required to support Marker and Pixmap Marker geometries. Provided a renderer supports updates to the camera->frustum transformation during the course of a scene, these geometries will be provided automatically by Quesa.

To improve performance it is recomended that plug-in renderers also support:

  • TriMesh
  • PolyLine

These geometries allow batches of triangles and lines to be submitted as a single object, which greatly reduces the per-primitive processing overhead.

Notes

Unlike QD3D, plug-in renderers running under Quesa can assume that the triangle and vertex normals of any submitted TriMeshes (in either retained or immediate mode) have been normalized. Normals associated with other geometric objects will not be normalized.

Plug-in metahandlers, and the methods they return, may be cached internally by Quesa. Under QD3D methods were always cached, however plug-ins should not rely on this behaviour when hosted by Quesa.
