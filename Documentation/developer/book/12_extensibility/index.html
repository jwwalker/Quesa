Plug-in Renderers

Notes

Unlike QD3D, plug-in renderers running under Quesa can assume that the triangle and vertex normals of any submitted TriMeshes (in either retained or immediate mode) have been normalized.

Normals associated with Triangle, or other geometries, may not be normalized.
