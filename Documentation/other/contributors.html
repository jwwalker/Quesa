Contributors

Quesa was started by Dair Grant, although substantial amounts of code have since been contributed by other developers. The direction of Quesa is set by the current project coordinator and the steering committee members (listed below).

Copyright to the source code of Quesa is shared between the developers who have contributed source code or resources to the project, in accordance with the LGPL.

Jeff Frey is currently a graduate student pursuing a Ph.D. in physical chemistry at the University of Delaware, with a focus on computational and theoretical chemistry.

His main involvement in the Quesa project was in the refinement and development of a few of the underlying mathematical routines. Jeff contributed to the project in hopes that he would then be able to continue work on his molecular rendering/modeling application THOR, which is built around the QuickDraw 3D graphics API.

When Apple discontinued QuickDraw 3D there seemed to be no future for 3D World. We planned to stop developing it after releasing version 3.1, throwing away 10 man years of development.

Now Quesa has become a reality we can continue to develop 3D World and our plug-in renderer with the knowledge that we now have full access to the source code of QuickDraw 3D.

I was very happy to contribute my 4 years I have been working with QuickDraw 3D into implementing Lights, Groups, Styles and the Viewer library.
