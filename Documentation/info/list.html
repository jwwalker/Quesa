Mailing List

The Quesa public mailing list is our primary forum for project coordination and discussion.

To send messages to the list, address them to quesa_list@designcommunity.com.

Subscribing and Unsubscribing

To join the Quesa list, just send email to majordomo@designcommunity.com, and in the body of the message say exactly: 

        subscribe quesa_list

To unsubscribe from the Quesa list, just send email to majordomo@designcommunity.com, and in the body of the message say exactly: 

        unsubscribe quesa_list

If your browser supports mailto links, you may be able to subscribe and unsubscribe from the list by following these links:

  • To subscribe to the list, click here.
  • To unsubscribe from the list, click here.

Check the body of the message before sending, just in case your mail client wasn't able to receive the full link.

Search List Archives

The mailing list archives can be searched with a full text search engine:

 

   

Detailed report     Simple report

Examples: 

        john and doe or jane
        john and (doe or jane)
        not (john or jane) and doe
        j* and doe
