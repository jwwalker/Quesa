Mailing Lists

The Quesa development mailing list, "quesa-develop", is our primary forum for project coordination and discussion. There is another mailing list, "quesa-cvs", that carries automatic announcements of changes committed to the CVS archive. See SourceForge to subscribe or browse the archives.

Forum

The on-line Quesa Forum is deprecated. Please note that posts to the forum are not copied automatically to the list. Since most of the developers are subscribed to the list rather than the forum, you may find that a post to the forum gets a quicker response if it is instead sent to the mailing list.
